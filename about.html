About Origin Guess!

Nichole Losh
Digital Marketing Major
Senior Innovation Project

What is Origin Guess!?

Origin Guess! is an interactive web-based game designed to teach people about AI by testing them on whether an image or text presented is AI or real. As artificial intelligence continues to expand everyday, it is becoming increasingly more difficult to tell what is authentic and what is not. Origin Guess! challenges users to critically think about what they see online and in their day to day life.

The Goal

The goal of Origin Guess! is to build up a generation that can detect AI and avoid it when they choose to. Building a sustainable future first starts with not buying into AI generated content, products, and scams. By detecting AI, Origin Guess! hopes to build a more authentic future for everyone.

Target Audience

Origin Guess! is primarily targeted to students, including:

  • Teenagers who frequently interact with images on social media
  • Students learning about digital literacy and exploring the rapidly growing world of technology
  • Anyone of any age curious about learning and expanding their knowledge

Because of the game’s interactive experience, it is accessible to both tech-savvy users and more casual users.

How it Works

The game consists of rounds in which players are given an image and must choose whether the image is AI-generated or real. After the player selects their answer, if it is correct they move on to the next round. If the answer is incorrect, the game provides context explaining the correct answer and highlights clues that reveal whether the image was AI-generated.

Players continue through ten rounds, and at the end the game displays a final score based on their answers. In future versions of the game, a leaderboard will allow players to compete with friends and track high scores.

What Makes Origin Guess! Different?

Origin Guess! is designed to be accessible, engaging, and easy to understand. While other platforms exist that discuss AI-generated media, many are either overly technical or not interactive.

Origin Guess! focuses on creating a simple and engaging experience that allows users to learn through gameplay. The platform also avoids political or adult content so it can be used by younger audiences without introducing unnecessary bias or sensitive material.

By combining education with gameplay, Reality Check! encourages users to become more aware of the images they consume in everyday life and to question whether what they are seeing is actually real.

Why Origin Guess!?

Origin Guess! was created after Nichole grew frustrated with her grandfather frequently sending AI-generated images and talking about how “cool” and “stunning” they were. This sparked the idea to create a project that helps people recognize AI-generated content and understand how easily it can spread online.

The goal is to help build a generation that can recognize AI-generated media, avoid scams, and become more digitally literate — while making the learning process fun and engaging.
