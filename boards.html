ADRO Feature Poster

Overview: Club article written for the UAT DND Club

What I Did: I had a questionnaire that I had the club leaders fill out that helped me to learn what exactly they wanted to present for their club. I then did my own research and put together an article that was understandable for both new people and current people looking for a new place to go.

Objective Met: By optimizing my SEO for this article, I expand its reach both inside and outside of UAT, allowing for anyone to come, read, and want to get involved.

Brain Activity into Direct Speech

Overview: This project takes SEO and uses it to promote a product that takes brain waves and turns it into direct speech.

What I Did: For this project, I took the SEO given and presented a project that optimizes the SEO and creates an infographic understandable for both current and new clients.

Objective Met: By using SEO, we can pull in new clients that would be interested in using this product.

Sports South Video

Overview: This video explains to a targeted viewer what is going on at an upcoming event.

Objective Met: This uses SEO optimization by introducing big music names and introducing an event that will get people excited. It is also used to show the brand's story and branding.

Barbie Movie Poster

Objective Met: This meets the engagement factor of this objective by creating an eye-catching poster to get people excited and wondering about the movie.
