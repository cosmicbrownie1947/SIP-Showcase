What is Origin Guess?

Origin Guess! is an interactive educational game designed to help players learn how to recognize artificial intelligence generated media. As AI continues to evolve, it becomes increasingly important for individuals to understand how to identify synthetic content in images, writing, and digital media.

Players are presented with a series of prompts and must determine whether the content was created by artificial intelligence or by a human. The goal is to build awareness, strengthen media literacy, and encourage critical thinking when interacting with digital content.

The game combines elements of education and entertainment to create a fast-paced experience where players can test their instincts and learn from instant feedback.
